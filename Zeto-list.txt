🔴 HIGH PRIORITY (Immediate Impact):-

	Favicon / Browser Tab Branding
		Issue: The favicon (tab icon) appears unclear/inconsistent and doesn’t align well with the brand 		identity.
		Impact:
			First impression feels slightly unpolished
			Reduces brand recall and visual clarity
		Suggestion:
			Use a simplified, high-contrast version of the logo
			Ensure proper scaling for small sizes (16x16, 32x32)

	Interaction Consistency (Custom Pointer)
		Issue: The custom pointer enlarges on hover and appears slightly inconsistent/blurry.
		Impact: Reduces precision and breaks the premium feel of the interface.
		Suggestion:
			Maintain a consistent pointer size
			Use color/state transitions instead of scaling
			Highlight the hovered element rather than the cursor

	Text Hierarchy & Readability
		Issue: Headings, subheadings, and body text lack strong visual differentiation.
		Impact: Reduces readability and makes content harder to scan.
		Suggestion:
			Increase heading contrast, size, or weight
			Slightly increase body text size
			Use softer/dimmer color for body text
			Improve vertical spacing between sections

	Content Scannability
		Issue: Sections are text-heavy and not easily scannable.
		Impact: Users may not quickly understand value propositions.
		Suggestion:
			Break content into smaller chunks
			Use bullet points / short highlights
			Introduce visual anchors (cards/icons/spacing)

	Primary CTA Clarity
		Issue: Multiple CTAs (e.g., “Book Strategy Call”, “Request Product Blueprint”) compete for 			attention.
		Impact: Creates decision friction and reduces conversions.
		Suggestion:
			Define a clear primary CTA
			Visually differentiate primary vs secondary actions
			Maintain consistency across sections

		Issue: Important stats or highlights are not strongly emphasized.
		Impact: Reduces impact of credibility signals.

		Issue: The website currently supports only a dark theme with no option for light mode.
		Impact: Limits usability for users who prefer light interfaces
